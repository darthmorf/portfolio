3D Printing & CAD

I enjoy 3D printing and CAD modelling as a hobby. I own a (modified) Ender 3 Pro, which has served me well for a few years now. I often design my own models in a whole range of categories, but mostly as containers for various electronics projects, such as my custom LED display. You can find some of the designs for things I have made that seem useful to others published to Thingiverse or Printables (I am more active on the latter). I hope to update this page soon with more detail on some of my projects!
